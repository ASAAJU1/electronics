 Arduino/Synapse RF Engine Shield:
===================================

All Through Hole parts for easy assembly for all skill levels
V-Reg and diode to step down 5v to 3.3v
Jumper selectable UARTs
Break out all Synapse pins
Extra break out pins for arduino

 Jumpers:
==========
1. 5/3.3V arduino
2. vreg disconnect
3. RFE UART0 RX UART1
4. RFE UART0 TX UART1
5. Arduino D0 RX D2
6. Arduino D1 TX D3
